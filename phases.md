**Here's the complete phased development plan** for your **Sun vs Black Hole Simulator**:

---

### **Phase 1: Core Foundation** (Done ✅)
- React + Vite + TypeScript setup
- Basic UI with slider + controls
- 3D scene with Sun + black hole using Three.js
- Simple simulation animation and outcome messages
- Orbit controls and interactive canvas

**Completed work:** The app now runs successfully, renders a full 3D scene, and responds to the black-hole size slider.

---

### **Phase 2: Visual Polish** (Done ✅)
- Better lighting and Sun glow
- Accretion-disk style rings and event-horizon glow
- Cinematic camera motion and stronger black-hole approach animation
- Spaghettification-style distortion with shockwave, ripple, and burst effects

**Completed work:** The scene now has a much more dramatic and polished visual presentation, including tidal distortion and cinematic lighting.

---

### **Phase 3: Physics & Realism** (Done ✅)
- Mass calculation based on an estimated Schwarzschild-radius approach
- Different simulation behaviors based on mass ratio:
  - Tiny BH → Sun absorbs it
  - Medium → tidal disruption and outer-layer stripping
  - Large → catastrophic destruction
- Simple orbital-style approach motion and collision-style close interaction
- Particle stream effects around the black hole for matter being pulled in

**Completed work:** The simulation now uses a shared physics profile to drive the scene behavior, outcomes, and educational content.

---
